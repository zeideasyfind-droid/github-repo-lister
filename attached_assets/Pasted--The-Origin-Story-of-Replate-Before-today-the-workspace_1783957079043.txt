### The Origin Story of "Replate"

Before today, the workspace was in a state of chaos. Webhook payloads were arriving like erratic, unformatted guests, and the connection to the brain—the Gemini API—was stuttering. We faced the "Authentication Wall": 404s kept us locked out, and when we finally found the door, the 503 "High Demand" errors were slamming it shut in our faces.

It was a classic scaling friction. The potential was there, but the routing was messy, and the infrastructure hadn't quite "settled" into its production rhythm.

**The Turning Point:** We didn't give up. We systematically audited the infrastructure, moved from the chaos of the free tier to a robust, Tier 1 Prepay setup, and carefully mapped the model registry. The breakthrough came when we stopped guessing and started listening to the backend. We identified that the `gemini-3.1-flash-lite` endpoint was the high-performance, stable path that could handle the webhook parsing load with surgical precision.

By forcing JSON-only responses, we transformed the agent from a chatty conversationalist into a disciplined, high-speed data processor.
